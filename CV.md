## Experimental Techniques

- **Imaging:** Cultured cell imaging (Calcium, Fura-2, and various sensors), 2-photon imaging with acute brain slice
- **Behavior:** Social behaviors (3-chamber test and five-trial social memory test), General behaviors (Open field test, novel object recognition and novel place recognition test), Fear-associated behavior (Passive avoidance test and contextual fear conditioning), Visuosocial memory test, AVATAR (Actnova)
- **Electrophysiology:** Patch clamping (Cultured cell and acute brain slice)
- **Molecular Biology:** Cloning, RT-PCR and Western blot
- **Surgery & Histology:** Primary astrocyte culture and microporation, Stereotaxic surgery (guide cannulation, microdialysis, drug infusion, virus injection), Immunohistochemistry (IHC) for mouse brain, Microdialysis (CMA microdialysis system)
- **Omics & Programming:** RNA-seq (Illumina HiSeq 2500), Computer programming (C++, Python, JavaScript, MATLAB), Homepage development (IBS behavior analysis center, https://subtle.ibs.re.kr/), Manual mouse behavior annotation program (KSPlayer)
